Our Vision

Building a safe, just, and sustainable Bangladesh where every child grows up secure, educated, and creative; where youth are empowered with technology-driven knowledge, leadership, humanity, and innovative thinking; where women are free from discrimination and sexual harassment, moving forward confidently‚Äîso we can collectively build a knowledge-based, humane, and inclusive society.

Our Mission

  • Empower children and youth to build a safe, knowledge-based society
  • Ensure protection of women and adolescents from discrimination and harassment
  • Build creative leadership through technology, education, and research
  • Engage youth and children in solving social and environmental issues
  • Present Bangladesh as a humane and innovative force internationally
